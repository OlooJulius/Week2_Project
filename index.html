This is a cultural way of naming children in GHANA.

About

Akan is the language of the people called akan.Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born

The table below shows a list of Akan names. If you can't quite recall the day of the week you were born, consider using the name generator.

List of Akan names.

Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
